Fitzpatrick, Joan. _Food in Shakespeare: Early Modern Dietaries and the Plays_. Ashgate, 2013.


Barnett, Eleanor “Reforming Food and Eating in Protestant England, c. 1560–c. 1640,” _The Historical Journal_ 63, no. 3 (2019): 507 - 527.


Burke, Peter. _Popular Culture in Early Modern Europe_. Routledge, 2016. 


Gentilcore, David. _Food and Health in Early Modern Europe Diet, Medicine and Society, 1450-1800._ Bloomsbury Academic, 2016. 


Fitzpatrick, Joan. _Renaissance Food from Rabelais to Shakespeare Culinary Readings and Culinary Histories_. Taylor and Francis, 2010. 


Flavin, Susan. _Consumption and Culture in Sixteenth-century Ireland: Saffron, Stockings and Silk _. Boydell Press, 2014. 


Lloyd, Paul S. _Food and Identity in England, 1540-1640: Eating to Impress_. Bloomsbury, 2016. 


Mennell, Stephen. _All Manners of Food: Eating and Taste in England and France from the Middle Ages to the Present_. University of Illinois                   Press, 2006. 


Thirsk, Joan. _Food in Early Modern England: Phases, Fads, Fashions, 1500-1760_. Continuum, 2006.
